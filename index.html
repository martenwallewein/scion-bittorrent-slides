Bittorrent over SCION - Original Setup

  • Target: Achieve 10Gbit/s bandwidth with Bittorrent over SCION
  • Achieved ~2.2Gbit/s bandwidth with OSR and Jumbo Frames (9000 byte)
  • With Bittorrent over TCP and parallel downloads ~8Gbit/s possible

Bottlenecks

  • SCION Dispatcher - Incoming and outgoing SCION traffic
  • Open Source Router - Slow packet forwarding
  • Client libraries - Slow packet processing
  • Quic-go - Limits performance of individual connections

Solutions

  • [x] SCION Dispatcher -> Bypass with eBPF/XDP
  • [x] Open Source Router -> Use High-Speed Router
  • [x] Client libraries -> OptimizedConnection
  • [?] Quic-go - Use alternative (PARTS, once it's ready)

New Benchmark Setup

  • 2x 10Gbit/s instead of 1Gbit/s + 10Gbit/s

Experiment 1: Bandwidth Aggregation

  • Multipath downloads over both available paths
  • Jumbo frames with MTU of 3000 (limit of XDP)

Experiment 2: Concurrent Downloads

  • Measured with jumbo frames enabled

Summary

  • Original results: maximum of 2.2 Gbit/s with MTU 9000
  • Current results: maximum of 8 Gbit/s with MTU 3000
  • Whats next: Replace quic-go with alternative transport protocol
